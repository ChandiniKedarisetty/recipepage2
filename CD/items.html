GULAB JAMUN ICECREAM

Ingredients:

  • Gulab jamun (ready-made or homemade)
  • Vanilla ice cream
  • Cardamom powder
  • Chopped nuts (like pistachios or almonds) for garnish
  • Rose water

Instructions

  1. Crumble the gulab jamun into small pieces.
  2. Mix the crumbled gulab jamun with softened vanilla ice cream
  3. Add a pinch of cardamom powder for flavor.
  4. Optionally, add a few drops of rose water for an aromatic touch.
  5. Gently mix everything together.
  6. Transfer the mixture into a container and freeze until solid.
  7. Serve garnished with chopped nuts if desired.
  8. Enjoy your delicious Gulab Jamun Ice Cream!
