BUTTER NAAN

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups (250 grams) all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon baking powder
  • ¼ teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 1 to 1½ teaspoons grated garlic
  • 2 tablespoons oil or melted butter
  • ½ cup yogurt
  • ¼ cup + 2 tablespoons lukewarm milk

Instructions

  1. Make the Dough
  2. Begin kneading the dough, adding more warm milk as needed. The dough should be soft and pliable
  3. Make the dough, then roll and toast the naans immediately
  4. They’ll be ready in under 30 minutes.
  5. Baking powder and baking soda are used as substitutes for yeast
  6. Divide the dough into small portions and roll them into circles.
  7. Flip and cook the other side until golden brown and brush with melted butter
