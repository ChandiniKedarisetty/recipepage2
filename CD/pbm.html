PANNER BUTTER MASALA

Ingredients:

  • Red and juicy tomatoes
  • Raw cashew
  • Cream
  • Spices
  • Salt and brown sugar
  • Panner

Instructions:

  1. Soak store-bought paneer in hot water for 15-20 minutes to soften it
  2. Saute onions, tomatoes, and cashews with spices until they are soft
  3. Blend the sauted mixture into a smooth puree to form the curry base
  4. In a pan, heat butter and add the gravy
  5. Add the paneer to the gravy and cook until they are heated through
  6. Stir in cream to make the curry rich and cream
